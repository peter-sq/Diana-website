A Message of Gratitude

Twenty years of service in God's vineyard is no small journey — it is twenty years of sacrifice, obedience, grace, and unwavering dedication.

Your life has been a light to many, a quiet strength, and a steady voice of wisdom. Through every season, you have poured yourself out in service, and heaven has surely taken note.

As you mark this milestone, I pray that the same God who called you will continue to direct your steps, renew your strength, and fill you with even greater wisdom and grace for the years ahead.

May the next chapters be greater than the former.

With Gratitude and Appreciation,
— Didi ✝
